Bathymetry of Active Volcanos

Working with Dr. Jeff Beeson and William Chadwick of Oregon State University, I cleaned and processed raw sonar data to create bathymetric maps and then difference grids for two active seamounts in the Mariana Arc (Ahyi and NW Rota-1).

I gained experience with MBARI MB-System, Qimera, and automating workflows in ArcGIS Pro using ModelBuilder and script tools.

Studying Precipitation in the Historic Record

Can we identify flash flood events using only precipitation data? I analyzed 100 years of monthly precipitation data to try doing just that. I used ArcPy scripts to clip and calculate statistics for 1,300 map layers, and organized my data in Excel. It kind of worked!! This was my first venture into using scripts to collect and analyze very large datasets. I am currently refining the workflow using R Studio.

Community Outreach Survey - Campus Bathroom Survey

For a class project, we were tasked with collecting our own spatial data. We sought public input on campus bathrooms: the good, the bad and the nasty. The project involved Survey123, ArcGIS Online, some Arc Pro and some good old fashioned outreach. The survey received over 250 responses.
