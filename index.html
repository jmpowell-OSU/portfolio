Studying Precipitation in the Historic Record

Can we identify flash flood events using only precipitation data? I analyzed monthly precipitation data from 1924-2024 to try doing just that. This was my first venture into utilizing scripts to collect and analyze very large datasets.

Campus Bathroom Survey

For a class project, we were tasked with collecting our own spatial data. Aiming to maximize responses, we sought public input on which campus bathrooms stink. The survey received over 250 responses.
